About Me

My name is Leonhard Kersten, and I am a Ph.D. Candidate at the Université Sorbonne Paris Nord and Visiting Scholar at the Center for the Integration of Science and Industry at Bentley University. I am a Researcher at the Academic-Industry Research Network and at the Berlin Centre for Empirical Economics at the Berlin School of Economics and Law. My research interests are corporate financialization, innovation dynamics, and public health outcomes.
